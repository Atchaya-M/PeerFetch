About Us

PeerFetch is your go-to platform for ordering and delivering stationery items within your college campus. We connect students who need supplies with peers who can deliver them quickly and for a nominal fee.

Our Mission

We aim to foster a community-driven solution for everyday stationery needs. PeerFetch empowers students to save time while creating a seamless delivery experience.

How It Works

1. Order

Place your order for stationery items through our platform.

2. Deliver

Fellow students deliver your order efficiently.

3. Earn

Delivery partners earn a nominal fee, ensuring mutual benefits.

Why choose us?

  • 1. Quick and convenient delivery.
  • 2. Affordable fees for students.
  • 3. Fosters campus community engagement.
